# Overview of Mexican Train

## Object of the Game
Play all tiles in your hand and go out each round.
When keeping score, the lowest score wins the game.

## First Player
Designating the **first player** is only important when house rules require someone to play or draw first in some situation.

### Designating the first player
1. Each player draws a tile, highest value is first.
    - **[0:0]** is the lowest value when blanks score 0
    - **[1:1]** is the lowest value when blanks score 25
1. Each player draws a tile, lowest value is first.
    - **[12:12]** is the highest value when blanks score 0
    - **[0:0]** is the highest value when blanks score 25
1. The youngest player at the table.
1. The oldest player at the table.
1. Next upcoming birthday
   - Today is December 31.  Player with birthday of January 1 is first.
1. Most Recent  birthday
   - Today is December 31.  Player with birthday of December 30 is first.

### When a first player is needed
- The Engine Tile is removed from the Boneyard and placed in the Depot before the Shuffle.
- When the Engine Tile for the round is not held by any player and must be drawn from the Boneyard.

    See [Place Engine](place-engine.md) for details.
